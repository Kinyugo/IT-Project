Cyber Security

Capital One Data Breach.

On July 19, 2019 Capital One realized that an external individual had gained access to their systems. The individual later identified as Paige Thompson, found a vulnerable web application with a weak fire wall, that allowed her to execute commands on Capital One’s server.

The hacker gained access to sensitive information, that had been collected between 2005 and 2019, pertaining to their credit cards. Around 100 million Americans and 6 million Canadians were affected by this data breach.
